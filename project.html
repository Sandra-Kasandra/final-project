Project Details

The "Flawless" project is a beauty and skincare website created as part of a school project. The primary goal was to design an elegant and user-friendly platform where users could explore beauty and skincare services. I used SASS for styling, focusing on making the site visually appealing while maintaining a clean and functional layout. HTML and JavaScript were used to ensure interactive features like forms and animations, and the final site was integrated into WordPress to make content management easier. All of the design was done by me in Figma, ensuring a unique and tailored look for the site.

  • Custom Design in Figma, providing a unique and modern aesthetic
  • Implemented SASS for clean, efficient styling and responsiveness
  • JavaScript used for interactive features like form validation and animations
  • WordPress integration for easy content updates and management
